About Us

Welcome To CITI PAWN

We have been in operation for 5 years as of 2019. Citi Pawn is a pawn shop on Ameshoff Street in Braamfontein's Hollard City Campus. We specialize in high-quality laptops, televisions, and cell phones as an authorized reseller. In addition, we repair and sell laptops and cell phones. We sell new and old laptops, televisions, and phones of all brands. We offer free on-the-spot personal exams and appraisals; no appointment is necessary. We invite you to bring your possessions in so that we can assess them, establish their market value, and acquire them right away if you desire to sell them. A free written evaluation for insurance purposes may be supplied.
